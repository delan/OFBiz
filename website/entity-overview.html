Common Application Components

The Common Application Components are pieces of the overall application that all businesses will need.  They are based on the general idea of a transaction.  There are four things involved in any transaction: a seller (person/company), a buyer (person/company), the item or service (product), and a records of the transaction (for the buyer and the seller).  These entities support this concept on both the buying (purchasing) and selling (sales) sides of the transaction.  All other parts of the overall application are centered around the Common Application Components.  All business systems will include these components to one extent or another in their applications, and the vertical applications are optional for many companies.
