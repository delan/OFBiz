OFBiz Training Videos

If you are trying to get started with OFBiz, or if you have been using OFBiz for a long time but still get the feeling that there are neat things you don't know about, these videos are for you!

There are two recorded training sessions included here, each available on a set of 3 DVDs with DivX encoded video (the codec to play them is included in the first disc).

The Feb 2004 Training Session done in Orem, UT (at the Undersun Consulting Offices there) include 3 days of Basic & Advanced Framework Training, with some material on the applications and data structure in the project (about 16 hours of video). Special thanks to Andrew Ballantine for his participating and support for this training. This is a higher quality recording at a standard DVD resolution (720x480), and is a one-on-one intensive training session.

The July 2004 Training Session done in Phuket, Thailand include 10 days of Comprehensive OFBiz Training and Exercises, covering nearly all of the material in the OFBiz Training Outlines (about 35 hours of video). Special thanks to SIPA (see www.sipa.or.th) for sponsoring and organizing this seminar, and to Hans Bakker for introducing OFBiz there and making this possible. This is a lower resolition (388x240) recording, but the text on the screen was typically very large and is still readable. This was a training seminar for 40 people and covers the material in a very generally applicable way and at a reasonable pace.

While these DVDs alone might be helpful, we recommend that in addition to viewing this material that you spent some time with an OFBiz Trainer to ask questions and get more detail on topics that are most important to you. Undersun Consulting LLC (where Andy & David's work) offers this for $120 per hour via email, phone, desktop sharing, online chat, or combinations of these means of communication. To complement these training videos we are offering a package including both the Framework Training from Feb 2004 and the Comprehensive Training from July 2004 (almost 50 hours of video), and 4 hours of remote time with an OFBiz Trainer (generally David Jones or Andy Zeneski) for $549.95, a savings of over $225! We recommend spending around 2 hours per day watching the videos and reviewing the material, taking about 4-8 weeks part time to finish the courses. The remote training hours must be used within 3 months of your order. This can be as effective as 5 days of on-site training, which for time and expenses would typically cost around $6,000, saving you over $5,450!

But what if I get the Complete Remote Training Package and the 4 hours are not enough to cover all of my questions and concerns? You can always buy additional remote training hours at $120 per hour, but we also have a supplemental hours package that gives you 10 hours of remote training for $999.95, saving you over $200!

Because the OFBiz Users Conference in April 2003 was so successful and popular, we decided to do it again in 2004! The May 2004 recording comes on 2 DVDs with high quality video in the DivX format (~14hours), and the April 2003 recording comes on 1 CD with webcast quality video in the DivX format.

As usual, the proceeds (except for expenses) will go directly to sponsoring new features and helping to support the Open For Business Project.

Seminars and Training

Seminars and Training Courses are being offered by Andy Zeneski and David E. Jones (through their consulting firm Undersun Consulting LLC) to spread knowledge of OFBiz and related topics. Revenue from the training will help to fund the open source project and improve the training materials and documentation. The training materials are available for free as part of the open source project, and are linked to in the training area to the left on this page. If you are interested in participating in these courses or seminars, please contact us at training@ofbiz.org.

In addition to organized training and seminars that are priced per participant we are also offering on-site training which can be customized if desired to your needs. This would be priced as a flat fee plus expenses per instructor rather than per attendee. Keep in mind that you are free to sell seats in the class to help reduce your cost if you choose this option. Once again if you are interested please contact us at training@ofbiz.org.

We also plan to offer online automated training offered as an ASP service. This will most likely be produced and operated by a partner that will provide the service and support and question answers as part of the price. Check back soon for more information on this.

On-Site Mentoring

Sometimes group training classes are not the most efficient or effective way to learn and do not provide as much one-on-one attention in spite of our efforts to do this as much as possible. For those who want help with analysis and design and then one-on-one assistance for team members as implementation begins we are offering an on-site mentoring service. The cost would be a flat rate plus expenses per "mentor" and personal attention would vary according to the number of team members taking advantage of the service.
