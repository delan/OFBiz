Services for The Open For Business Project

Project on Source Forge: The Open For Business Project (ofbiz)

Founders and Lead Architects: David E. Jones & Andy Zeneski

The purpose of this area of the site is to provide references to those who are providing services based on the Open For Business Project. These services may include hosting, training, installation, support, custom development, design, analysis and other related services.

To be listed here please contact David E. Jones (jonesde@ofbiz.org). Contributors to the project can be listed here for free. Others can be listed for a fee depending on the type and volume of services you wish to provide.

The founders of the Open For Business Project, David E. Jones (data sheet) and Andy Zeneski, have also founded a consulting organization. This organization offers services such as strategy, analysis, training, architecture, custom development, installation, and configuration. Services such as hosting and support are in the planning stages. Hourly and fixed rate services may be negotiated. Because we are interested in the open source project reduced rates will be given for effort that can be contributed back to the project. If you are interested and would like more information on available services, schedules and rates, contact David E. Jones (jonesde@ofbiz.org) or Andy Zeneski (jaz@zsolv.com).
