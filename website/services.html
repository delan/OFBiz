OFBiz Reference Books

The OFBiz Reference Book series will include references for the OFBiz Framework, the OFBiz Data Model (entities) and the OFBiz Services. In the future other references may be added, such as page flow diagrams (ie from the Control Servlet config files), and so on.

The first reference book available is the OFBiz Framework Reference Book. This book is 16 pages (8 double-sided 8.5x11 sheets) and contains a complete reference of all OFBiz XML files, plus the main configuration properties files. The first page in the book presents development and configuration diagrams that help as an overview of the organization of the book.

Of course, rather than trying to describe in detail what is in the book, it might be better to just show you! Here is a list of the book pages, and thumbnails of all 16 pages in the book, with more detailed images available for some of the pages so you can get an idea of what is included. Of course, these are just lower resolution images of the real stuff and don't scale or print very well.

This valuable quick reference will help you develop faster and more effectively from the first day you use it, and every day you use it. It is ideal for everyone you does OFBiz development and/or configuration, from beginners and newcomers to OFBiz to the most seasoned and experienced OFBiz developers (even the founders of OFBiz use them!).

Seminars and Training

Seminars and Training Courses are being offered by Andy Zeneski and David E. Jones (through their consulting firm Undersun Consulting LLC) to spread knowledge of OFBiz and related topics. Revenue from the training will help to fund the open source project and improve the training materials and documentation. The training materials are available for free as part of the open source project, and are linked to in the training area to the left on this page. If you are interested in participating in these courses or seminars, please contact us at training@ofbiz.org.

In addition to organized training and seminars that are priced per participant we are also offering on-site training which can be customized if desired to your needs. This would be priced as a flat fee plus expenses per instructor rather than per attendee. Keep in mind that you are free to sell seats in the class to help reduce your cost if you choose this option. Once again if you are interested please contact us at training@ofbiz.org.

We also plan to offer online automated training offered as an ASP service. This will most likely be produced and operated by a partner that will provide the service and support and question answers as part of the price. Check back soon for more information on this.

On-Site Mentoring

Sometimes group training classes are not the most efficient or effective way to learn and do not provide as much one-on-one attention in spite of our efforts to do this as much as possible. For those who want help with analysis and design and then one-on-one assistance for team members as implementation begins we are offering an on-site mentoring service. The cost would be a flat rate plus expenses per "mentor" and personal attention would vary according to the number of team members taking advantage of the service.
