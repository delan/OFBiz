The purpose of this area of the site is to provide references to those who are providing services based on the Open For Business Project. These services may include hosting, training, installation, support, custom development, design, analysis and other related services.

The OFB Group has been formed to handle the quality control and maintenance tasks associated with keeping a list of approved Open For Business services providers. The OFB Group itself does not offer services and does not have technical control over the Open For Business Project. Its only purpose is to certify affiliated services organization.
