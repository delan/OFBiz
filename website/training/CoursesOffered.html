Policies:
  • BYOL: Bring Your Own Laptop, or Rent for $300
  • Two (2) Weeks Advance Registration
  • After two weeks before the course there will be a 20% late charge for registration.
  • $100 Cancellation Charge if you cancel at least one week before the course starts (no cancellation after one week before).
  • If we cancel the course a 100% refund will be issued. Courses will be finalized or cancelled at the latest 1 week before the start of the course.
  • The city location will announced well in advance but the actual training location may not be finalized until 1 week before the class starts.
  • The course will always be done if there are at least 4 people registered, otherwise we will have to decide based on other factors.
