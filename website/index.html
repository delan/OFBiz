Introduction

The Open For Business Project, founded in 2001 by David E. Jones and Andy Zeneski, is an open source enterprise automation software project licensed under the MIT Open Source License. The goal of the project is to build a community of end users and developers that work together directly to create easy to customize business software based on best practices.

Use Open For Business to automate your business information and process management in your own unique way by customizing high quality open source applications instead of building everything yourself.
