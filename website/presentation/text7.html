The OFBiz Data Model

  • Parallels the OFBiz application goal of being generally useful for most businesses

  • Highly normalized to meet requirements without excessive duplication of data

  • Originally based on The Data Model Resource Book by Len Silverston (John Wiley & Sons)

  • Make heavy use of the Extensibility Pattern

  • Currently there are over 425 Entities
